Mitype

Mitype is a program to test (and hence improve) your typing speed right from the ease of your terminal.

Main Features

  • 🖥️ Cross-platform

  • 🎦 See your replay

  • 🐍 Supports Python 2 and 3

  • 📝 Choose custom text input

  • 🅰️ 6000 text samples

  • 🌈 Colored texts
